Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 2 large tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tablespoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Method

1. Prepare the Gravy:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and fry for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn mushy and oil separates.
  5. Blend this mixture into a smooth paste and return it to the pan.

2. Cook the Paneer:

  1. Add the paneer cubes to the gravy and cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  2. Add garam masala, kasuri methi, and fresh cream. Mix well and simmer for another 2 minutes.

3. Serve:

  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
