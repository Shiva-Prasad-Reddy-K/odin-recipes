Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken Tikka:

  • 500 grams chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

For the Masala Gravy:

  • 2 large onions (chopped)
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tablespoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Method

1. Marinate the Chicken:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, salt, and oil.
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and coat well. Marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight).

2. Cook the Chicken Tikka:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Place the marinated chicken pieces on a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked and slightly charred.
  3. You can also cook the chicken on a grill or stovetop until done.

3. Prepare the Masala Gravy:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Fry for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.

4. Combine the Chicken and Gravy:

  1. Add the cooked chicken tikka pieces to the gravy and stir well.
  2. Add garam masala, kasuri methi, and fresh cream. Mix and simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat.

5. Serve:

  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
